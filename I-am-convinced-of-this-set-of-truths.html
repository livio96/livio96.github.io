I am convinced of this set of truths

  1. How you do anything is how you do everything.
  2. Taking a step back to rethink looks like is slowing you down, but continuing in the wrong path, will slow you down 10x more.
  3. If you have to choose between time and money, choose the first. Money can be remade, time cannot.
  4. You will change, and not necessarily because you want to but because the world around you changes.
  5. The best part of a plan is plan B.
  6. Never risk something you have and you need for something that you don’t have and don’t need.
  7. There is no overnight success but for sure there is overnight failure.
  8. Top 2 financial strategies: Live below your means, and don’t try to impress anyone.
  9. Be careful what you consider a good advice. Not everyone is playing the same game as you.
  10. Consider yourself to be a genius, if you can do the average thing when everyone else around you is losing his mind.
  11. You will really get to know someone, when you will see how they react in a stressful situation.
  12. The easy way is always more costly.
  13. Always be aware of hidden costs, cause those are the hardest to calculate.
  14. Most important decision in life : who you marry.
  15. Best inventions happen when nobody is paying attention.
  16. Sometimes the “laziest” time of the day can turn out to be the most productive. Enhanced creativity kicks in when your mind is clear.
